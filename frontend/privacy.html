Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Internship Tracker only requests read-only Gmail access to identify and classify internship-related emails. We do not send, modify, or delete email content.

Data We Access

We access email metadata and message content needed to classify application outcomes (for example: no response, rejected, online assessment).

How Data Is Used

Data is used only to display your dashboard results. Tokens are stored on the backend to keep your session connected to Gmail.
